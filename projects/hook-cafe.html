Hook Cafe

A web app encouraging social interaction

The Concept

The idea is to quickly find a colleague to join you for lunch. Send the message in the morning where|when you are available and what your interests are. The app should help you to find someone "similar" in your area. The app can be useful if you are traveling to a new place.

Implementation

The app MVP works on the web and its PWA. It runs on nostr protocol and was built in svelte.
